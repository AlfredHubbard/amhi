Oregon Psilocybin Facilitator Training Program
Approved by the Oregon Health Authority

AMHI’s Psychedelic Facilitator Bootcamp December 2025

The Alfred M. Hubbard Institute educates psychedelic facilitators to work in Oregon psilocybin service centers or similar settings elsewhere. Oregon Psilocybin Services (OPS) is a section of the Oregon Health Authority. OPS approved AMHI’s curriculum as meeting the requirements for our graduates to apply for a psilocybin facilitator license.

AMHI is now accepting applications from students who wish to study during the final class of 2025. Our facilitation bootcamp is an intensive three-week program that will begin in the second week of Decemeber and end shortly after Christmas. Some of our students plan to practice outside of Oregon, so it is inconventient for them to travel to Oregon for their practicum. We will conduct our practicum in the San Francisco Bay Area. After New Years 2026, OPS will require all practicums to be conducted in Oregon service centers, which charge a hefty $3,000 per student.
